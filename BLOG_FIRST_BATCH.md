### 5. Builder Warranty Spain 10 Year
**Slug**: `builder-warranty-spain-10-year`
**Keywords**: builder warranty spain, 10 year guarantee spain, structural warranty new build
**Quick Answer**: Spanish law requires a 3-tier warranty on new builds: 1 year for finishing defects, 3 years for habitability issues (plumbing, electrical), and 10 years for structural problems. Developers must provide insurance (seguro decenal) covering the 10-year structural guarantee. Keep all documentation.
