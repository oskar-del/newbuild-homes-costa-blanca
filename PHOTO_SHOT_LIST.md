## ğŸ“± PHOTO SPECIFICATIONS

### Technical Requirements
| Type | Dimensions | Format | Max Size |
|------|------------|--------|----------|
| Hero/Banner | 2400x1000px min | WebP | 500KB |
| Article header | 1600x900px | WebP | 300KB |
| In-article | 1200x800px | WebP | 200KB |
| Thumbnail | 600x400px | WebP | 50KB |
| Panorama | 3000x1000px+ | WebP | 600KB |

### Naming Convention
```
{category}-{location}-{subject}-{number}.webp

Examples:
beach-torrevieja-playa-del-cura-01.webp
golf-villamartin-clubhouse-01.webp
lifestyle-market-produce-01.webp
hero-costa-blanca-sunset-01.webp
```

### Style Guidelines
- **Bright & Optimistic**: Mediterranean sunshine, blue skies
- **People Optional**: Include people for lifestyle, exclude for properties
- **Modern Feel**: Contemporary, not dated tourism shots
- **Authentic**: Real scenes, not overly staged
- **Consistent Color**: Warm Mediterranean palette
- **High Quality**: Sharp, well-exposed, professional level
